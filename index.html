Software for Social Good

We are a multi-disciplinary team of Cal Poly students, dedicated to providing valuable software to nonprofit organizations.

Our Work

Each year, we collaborate with 2-4 nonprofit organizations and address an issue that each organization faces. After identifying solutions that would address these issues, we organize teams of 8-12 student developers to develop innovative software applications that serve to assist each nonprofit in reaching their goals. This past year, we built a data visualization dashboard for Carelink, mental health nonprofit based in Pennsylvania. We're currently working on projects for CASA of San Luis Obispo and the Central Coast Autism Spectrum Center.
